Peak Services

Home Exterior,
Structure & Roof

From your gutters down to your floors — we handle every aspect of your home's upkeep so you can focus on living in it, not maintaining it.

Gutter Clearing & Downspout Flushing

Clogged gutters cause costly water damage. We clear every inch of debris and flush downspouts to keep rainwater flowing safely away from your foundation.

High-Reach Window & Screen Cleaning

Crystal-clear windows from the inside out, including hard-to-reach upper floors. We clean glass and screens to let the light back in — streak-free guaranteed.

Exterior Pressure Washing & Soft Washing

Blast away grime, mildew, and stains from siding, driveways, decks, and fences. We choose the right wash method for every surface — powerful yet gentle.

Interior Painting & Sheetrock Repairs

Holes, dents, cracks, or a full room refresh — our team patches and paints with clean lines and minimal disruption, leaving walls looking factory-fresh.

Caulking — Windows, Kitchens & Bathrooms

Old or cracked caulk lets in moisture, mold, and drafts. We re-seal every gap around windows, tubs, showers, and sinks with a clean, watertight finish.

Professional Floor Cleaning & Carpet Shampooing

Deep-clean hard floors and shampoo carpets to remove embedded dirt, stains, and allergens. Your floors will look — and feel — brand new again.

TV Mounting & Clean Wire Concealment

Get your TV perfectly positioned with no dangling cables in sight. We mount on any wall type and hide all wiring for a polished, built-in look.

Faucet, Toilet & Garbage Disposal Replacements

Dripping faucets, running toilets, or a disposal that won't spin — we swap out worn fixtures quickly with quality parts and zero mess left behind.

Minor Plumbing Leak Repairs

Catch drips before they become floods. We fix minor leaks under sinks, behind toilets, and at supply lines — fast, affordable, and properly sealed.

Professional Furniture Assembly

Skip the instructions headache. Our team assembles furniture from any major brand — beds, desks, shelves, and more — quickly and correctly, every time.

All Minor Handyman Tasks & Home Repairs

That ever-growing to-do list? We'll knock it out. Door adjustments, cabinet hardware, light fixture swaps, weatherstripping, shelving — if it needs fixing, we handle it. One call, one crew, no need to juggle multiple contractors.
